Overview

I was admitted to, and successfully completed the 2019 cohort for the Policy in Action program, at Young Leaders Active Citizenship which is designed to build core skills in policy analysis, advocacy, and public leadership. In a span of 3 months, I delivered a bill draft for promoting tourism in the state of Arunachal Pradesh in tandem with Ninong Ering, a Member of Parliament. This live research project included a thorough study of the current issues that tourism in Arunachal Pradesh faces and the potential that this industry holds. It is sesitive information, so I am unable to speak more about it here. To learn more about this project or access the full bill, feel free to reach out to me.
